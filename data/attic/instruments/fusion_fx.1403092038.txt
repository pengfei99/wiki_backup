====== Fusion FX ======

===== Introduction =====

The FUSION-FX can image both chemiluminescence and fluorescence applications. For fluorescence, the Fusion FX system is easily upgradeable to the LED Spectra module for blue, green, near infrared or infrared illumination.The Spectra module is ideal for a large array of applications such as Western blot chemiluminescence, Western blot fluorescence, 1D DNA gel, 1D protein colorimetric samples, stain-free gel and blot.\\
