====== MacsQuant VYB ======

===== Introduction =====

With the MACSQuant VYB you can expect all of the performance, automation and convenience of the MACSQuant Analyzer but with an optical layout configured with a yellow laser. As the availability of new antibodies and fluorochromes has expanded in recent years, so has the demand for flow cytometers that are configured to detect these colors. By incorporating a 561nm laser and eight fluorescent channels the MACSQuant VYB instrument is an ideal choice for analysis of fluorescent proteins such as mCherry, DsRed, GFP, CFP and other fruit fluorescent proteins. In addition, the configuration is optimized for distinction of PE and FITC fluorochromes, thus allowing improved detection of dim PE populations.
