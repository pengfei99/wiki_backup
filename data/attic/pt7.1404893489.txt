====== Plateau Technique no 7 : Métabolomique et Protéomique ======


The first goal of the Metabolomics core facility, is to develop innovative analytical approaches in metabolomics, in the fields of infectiology and microbiology, that will be used for R&D projects of BIOASTER, in collaboration with the academic community and industrial partners.
